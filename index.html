Hao Sun

Hello! I am Hao Sun, a researcher specializing in Artificial Intelligence (AI). I earned my Ph.D. from Zhejiang University in China, focusing on multimodal learning, and I am currently affiliated with Ritsumeikan University in Japan.

My research interests encompass multimodal learning, large-scale models, reinforcement learning, sentiment analysis, and related fields. I am particularly passionate about the advancement of Artificial General Intelligence (AGI) and its correlated technologies, aiming to push the boundaries of and human civilization.

I have published several research papers in esteemed journals, including Information Fusion and IEEE Transactions on Affective Computing. For more details on my research and publications, please visit my Google Scholar page or ORCID page.
